◆ Flat

template<typename RingLike>
FlatBoundaryMatrix Flat

A vector of vectors representing the fully indexed flat boundary matrix. For example, the \(d\)th entry contains the indices of its \((d-1)\)-dimensional faces.

◆ Full

template<typename RingLike>
SparseMatrix<RingLike> Full

A SparseMatrix representing the full boundary matrix \(\partial\) for the complex \(X\) — that is, the square matrix with rows and columns indexed by all cells of \(X\) where \(\partial_{ij} = \pm 1\) if cell \(i\) is a face of cell \(j\), and \(0\) otherwise.
