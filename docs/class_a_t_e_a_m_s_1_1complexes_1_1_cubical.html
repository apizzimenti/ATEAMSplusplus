◆ corners

template<typename T = ATEAMS::ff>
std::vector<int> ATEAMS::complexes::Cubical< T >::corners

Corners of the lattice.

If corners is \((c_1, c_2, \dots, c_n)\), then the integer lattice created is the product \( \prod [0, c_i] \); if periodic boundary conditions are imposed, opposite vertices (and thus opposite \((d-1)\)-faces) are identified to form an \(n\)-fold torus.
