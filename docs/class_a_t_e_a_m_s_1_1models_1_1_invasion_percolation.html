Implements the plaquette invasion-percolation algorithm, which creates a random \(d\)-subcomplex \(X\) by (uniformly randomly) inserting \(d\)-cells into \(X\) until \(\rank(H_d(X))\) is "large enough" (set by the user).

Parameters
tTime step.
optionsMultithreaded computing environment options.
Returns
A list of \(d\)-cell indices in insertion order, up to the time at which \(\rank(H_d(X))\) exceeds the user-specified cutoff.
