template<typename RingLike>
class ATEAMS::models::SwendsenWang< RingLike >

Implements the Swendsen–Wang algorithm for Potts lattice gauge theory (PLGT) and the plaquette random-cluster model (PRCM).

Model sampler.

Alternates between sampling the conditionals \(P_t \sim \PK(- \mid f_t)\) and \(f_{t+1} \sim \PK(- \mid P_{t+1})\) of the coupling \(\PK\) between the PLGT and PRCM.
